## Cowork — AI Agents That Work Alongside You

**RickyTicky BobbyWobbin is more than a chat client.** It's a Cowork platform where AI agents work alongside you on your computer — reading files, writing code, browsing the web, and automating tasks. You see everything the agent does, and you're always in control.
